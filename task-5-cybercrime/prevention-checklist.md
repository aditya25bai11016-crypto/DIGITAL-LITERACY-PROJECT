# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

**For College Students in India**

---

## ✅ Online Safety Tips

* Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts (email, social media, college portals, etc.).
* Never click on suspicious links sent through unknown emails, WhatsApp messages, or social media.
* Always check the website address before entering login details. Fake websites often look very similar to real ones.
* Do not share personal information such as Aadhaar number, OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone online.
* Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for your email, Instagram, and other important accounts.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts or making payments.
* Do not download apps or files from unknown websites.
* Think before posting photos, personal information, or private details online.

---

## 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
* Always check the name of the receiver before sending money through UPI.
* Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.
* If someone asks you to “receive money” by entering your UPI PIN, it is a fraud.
* Immediately block your bank account if you notice any suspicious transaction.

---

## 🚨 Where to Report Cybercrime in India

If you become a victim of online fraud or cybercrime, report it immediately:

**National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in
**Cybercrime Helpline Number:** 1930

---

**Conclusion:**
Following these simple safety steps can help college students stay safe online and protect their personal and financial information.
