## Project Overview

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio created as part of the CSE0001 – Digital Literacy course. The objective of this project is to understand how to use digital platforms responsibly, build a professional online presence, communicate effectively through email, and stay safe from cybercrime.

The project is divided into five tasks, each based on one module of the course. All the required files, screenshots, and documents have been organized in separate folders according to the instructions provided in the assignment brief.

## Task Summary

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

In this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using an online design tool. The infographic explains the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and the importance of a professional online presence.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio

I created and updated my professional profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional digital identity and how these platforms can help in internships and career development.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms

I created an account on a coding practice platform and completed a beginner-level challenge. I also created a Google Form for a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz and added screenshots of the form and response sheet.

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Social Media Checklist

In this task, I wrote two professional emails: one requesting an assignment extension and another applying for a summer internship. I also created a Social Media Do’s and Don’ts checklist for responsible online behaviour.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study

I wrote a case study on phishing and created a “Stay Safe Online” checklist with practical safety tips for college students in India. This task helped me understand how cybercrimes happen and how they can be prevented.
