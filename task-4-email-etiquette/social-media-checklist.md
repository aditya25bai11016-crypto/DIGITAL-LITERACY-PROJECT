# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

**Responsible Social Media Use for College Students**

---

## ✅ Do's

* Use social media in a positive and respectful way.
* Think before posting anything online.
* Keep your personal information private.
* Use strong passwords and protect your accounts.
* Share useful and meaningful content.
* Respect others’ opinions and viewpoints.
* Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts.

---

## ❌ Don'ts

* Do not post offensive or harmful content.
* Do not share fake news or unverified information.
* Do not bully or make fun of others online.
* Do not share personal details like phone number, address, or passwords.
* Do not post anything that can affect your future career.
* Do not use social media during important study time.
* Do not argue aggressively in comments or messages.

---

**Conclusion:**
Responsible use of social media helps students maintain a positive image, stay safe online, and use digital platforms in a productive way.
